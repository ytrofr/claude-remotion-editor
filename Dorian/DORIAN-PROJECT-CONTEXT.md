## 1. Product Overview

### What is Dorian?
**Dorian** (formerly "Moly") is an **AI-based community marketplace** platform. The tagline is "Community Marketplace".

### Core Value Proposition
A one-stop platform connecting three user types:

| User Type | Pain Point | Dorian Solution |
|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| **Consumers** | Hard to find relevant products at fair prices with fast delivery | Discover products tailored to interests, themes, or location using AI |
| **Vendors/SMBs** | Struggle to compete against giant brands | Boost sales by reaching targeted audiences with AI insights |
| **Community Leaders** | Seeking ways to monetize communities | Marketplace connecting vendors and buyers based on follower interests |

### Two Discovery Modes
1. **DORIAN.local** - Discover and purchase from nearby local stores (location-based)
2. **DORIAN.marketplace** - Discover products from communities based on interests (theme-based)

### AI Assistant
The platform features "Dorian" - a personal AI shopping assistant that:
- Customizes the shopping experience based on user data and preferences
- Helps users find and purchase products through natural conversation
- Greeting: "Hi! I am Dorian your personal assistant, how can I help you?"

## 2. Key Features

### For Consumers
- Personalized experience tailored to location + interests
- AI-powered product discovery and recommendations
- Community-based shopping (hobby groups, themes)
- Nearby store discovery with fast delivery

### For Vendors
- **One Click Marketing** - AI generates campaigns and ads tailored to target audience
- **Data-based BI Insights** - Smart reports and action items from store data
- **Automated Customer Relationship** - AI manages marketing and remarketing
- **Smart Catalog Management** - AI-powered category organization
- **SEO Blog Writing** - AI-generated content
- Built-in analytics (no Google Analytics needed)

### For New Stores
- Create store from scratch with AI automation
- Auto-generate: product names, prices, descriptions, categories, images
- Natural language commands ("Prepare reports", "Create marketing creatives")

### Rating System (Priority Pyramid)
| Tier | Name | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| 1 | Close & Interesting | Best match: user preferences AND proximity |
| 2 | Close OR Interesting | Match by proximity OR interests |
| 3 | Discover | New products to build personal rating |
