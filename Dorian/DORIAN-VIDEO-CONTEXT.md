## Product Overview

**Dorian** is an AI-based community marketplace platform that connects:
- **Consumers** - Find products tailored to interests, themes, or location
- **Vendors/SMBs** - Boost sales by reaching targeted audiences with AI insights
- **Community Leaders** - Monetize communities through marketplace connections

### Tagline
"Community Marketplace" - A new shopping experience

### Key Differentiators
1. **Location-Based Discovery** (DORIAN.local) - Buy from nearby local stores
2. **Interest-Based Discovery** (DORIAN.marketplace) - Products from communities matching your interests
3. **AI Assistant (Dorian)** - Personal shopping assistant that customizes the experience
4. **Store Management Tools** - AI-powered vendor dashboard
5. **Data Insights** - Actionable analytics for vendors, consumers, and community leaders

### Rating System (Priority Pyramid)
1. **Close & Interesting** - Best match: user preferences + proximity
2. **Close OR Interesting** - Match by proximity OR interests
3. **Discover** - New products to build personal rating
